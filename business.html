The 3Ps Framework: People, Property, Planet

Our governance approach integrates three interconnected pillars to support sustainable operations and duty of care obligations.

People

Workforce health, safety, and wellbeing programs that support compliance with Work Health and Safety (WHS) legislation and duty of care requirements. Includes incident management, contractor oversight, and competency frameworks.

Property

Asset integrity, operational risk management, and multi-site governance. Covers equipment management, maintenance scheduling, facility compliance, and infrastructure monitoring aligned to AS/NZS ISO 31000 and industry standards.

Planet

Environmental monitoring, regulatory compliance, and sustainability governance. Supports alignment with ISO 14001 environmental management principles, including emissions tracking, waste management, and environmental incident response.

How We Engage

Three service pathways designed to meet organisations at different stages of governance maturity.

Advisory

Strategic guidance and compliance support from experienced HSE professionals.

  • Gap analysis and risk assessments
  • Management system design (ISO 45001, ISO 14001)
  • Legislative compliance reviews
  • Board-level reporting and governance frameworks

For: Executive teams, HSE managers, compliance officers

Systems

Technology platforms and tools to centralise safety and compliance data.

  • Incident management and reporting
  • Real-time monitoring (IoT sensors, wearables)
  • Document control and audit trail management
  • Integration with operational workflows

For: Operations teams, site managers, frontline supervisors

Assurance

Independent verification, audits, and continuous improvement programs.

  • Internal and external audits
  • Compliance verification against standards
  • Third-party assessments
  • Benchmarking and performance tracking

For: Quality assurance teams, audit managers, regulators
