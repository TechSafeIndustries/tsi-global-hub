# TechSafe Industries — Commercial Tiers (Internal)

## Tier 1 — Governance Foundation

### Who it's for
- Single-site or small multi-site operations
- Organisations moving off spreadsheets or fragmented tools
- Buyers seeking defensible safety governance first

### Includes
- TechSafeAI Core Governance Engine
  - Incident management (ICAM-aligned)
  - Action tracking with approvals
  - Audit and inspection workflows
  - Evidence and audit trails
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Approval gates
- AU/NZ-aligned templates
- Board-ready reporting (monthly)

### Explicitly excludes
- Smart Tools
- DigiBoards
- Automation claims

### Positioning
"Establish authority, evidence, and control before scale."

--------------------------------

## Tier 2 — Governed Operations

### Who it's for
- Multi-site operations
- Contractor-heavy environments
- Organisations under regulator or board scrutiny

### Includes
Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Delegated authority and escalation rules
- Critical Control Verification (CCV)
- Contractor governance
- Cross-site reporting (site / region / group)
- Integration-ready inputs (Smart Tools supported, not required)

### Optional add-ons
- Smart Tools (Inputs Pack)
- DigiBoards (Visibility Pack)
- Advisory onboarding services

### Positioning
"Run operations with governed visibility and accountability."

--------------------------------

## Tier 3 — Enterprise Assurance System

### Who it's for
- Major Hazard Facilities
- Mining majors, Oil & Gas operators, airports
- Boards requiring continuous assurance

### Includes
Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Enterprise assurance workflows
- Executive dashboards
- Regulator export bundles
- DigiBoards (approved visibility layer)
- Multi-entity / multi-tenant support
- Formal assurance artefacts

### Mandatory rules
- DigiBoards cannot exist without Tier 3 governance
- No visibility without approval

### Positioning
"Continuous assurance across people, property, and operations."
