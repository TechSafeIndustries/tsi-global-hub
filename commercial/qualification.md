## Tier 1 Indicators
- Single site
- Manual or spreadsheet-based safety systems
- Limited contractor complexity
- Governance gaps identified

## Tier 2 Indicators
- Multiple sites
- Contractor-heavy operations
- Repeated audit findings
- Board or regulator visibility required

## Tier 3 Indicators
- Major Hazard Facility
- Critical infrastructure
- Continuous assurance required
- Executive or regulator reporting obligations

## Disqualifiers / Red Flags
- Requests for autonomous decision-making
- Desire for visibility without governance
- "Real-time automated response" requirements

## Core Rule
No automation without oversight.
Governance always precedes visibility.
