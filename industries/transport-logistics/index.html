The Transport & Logistics Reality

Common failure modes in transport & logistics today

  • Rail possessions and signalling/track access not consistently governed
  • Vessel interface and lifting ops across port-rail-road handovers fragmented
  • Permit-to-Work and emergency readiness conducted retrospectively
  • Contractor control lost across multi-modal and intermodal operations
  • Executives lack defensible visibility across distributed operations

This describes risk exposure, not technology failure.

Example Governed Transport & Logistics Workflows

Rail: Possessions & Signalling/Track Access

  • Possession requested with scope and interface risk assessment

Shipping/Ports: Vessel Interface & Lifting Ops

Intermodal: Rail ? Port ? Road Handovers

  • No uncontrolled closure or escalation
