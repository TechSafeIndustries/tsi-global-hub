The Construction Reality

Common failure modes in construction today

  • Incidents captured but not consistently closed
  • Corrective actions lost across sites and contractors
  • Assurance conducted retrospectively, not continuously
  • Safety data fragmented across tools, spreadsheets, and emails
  • Executives lack defensible, real-time visibility

This describes risk exposure, not technology failure.

How TechSafe Governs Construction Operations

TechSafeAI acts as the system of record for Construction safety and compliance.

Governed Capabilities

  • Incident capture and ICAM-aligned investigations
  • Corrective actions with ownership, approvals, and evidence
  • Critical control verification (CCV)
  • Audit and inspection workflows aligned to AU/NZ expectations
  • Immutable evidence and decision trails

Outcome: Decisions are traceable, defensible, and regulator-ready.

Example Governed Construction Workflows

Incident → Closure

  • Incident logged (mobile/web)
  • Severity and consequence assessed
  • Investigation conducted with evidence
  • Actions assigned, approved, and closed
  • Full incident-to-close audit trail retained

Critical control Assurance

  • Control verification scheduled or triggered
  • Evidence required before closure
  • Escalation where controls fail
  • Executive-ready assurance reporting

SubSubcontractor Oversight

  • Contractor incidents and actions governed under site authority
  • Role-based access enforced
  • No uncontrolled closure or escalation
