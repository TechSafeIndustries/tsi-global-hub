The Energy & Renewables Reality

Common failure modes in energy & renewables today

  • Incidents across remote wind, solar, and battery sites not consistently closed
  • Working at heights and electrical safety governance fragmented across contractors
  • Environmental compliance and assurance conducted retrospectively
  • Corrective actions lost across distributed and remote operations
  • Executives lack defensible visibility across multi-site portfolios

This describes risk exposure, not technology failure.
