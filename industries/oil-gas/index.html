The Oil & Gas Reality

Common failure modes in oil & gas today

  • Loss of containment and process safety risk
  • SIMOPS conflicts (simultaneous operations)
  • Permit-to-Work and isolation failures
  • Contractor-heavy operations across shifts
  • Offshore/onshore emergency readiness gaps

This describes risk exposure, not technology failure.
