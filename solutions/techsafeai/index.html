Core Capabilities

What clients buy: governed workflows with defensible outcomes.

1. Incident Management (System of Record)

Purpose: Capture, govern, and close incidents with evidence integrity.

  • Guided incident capture (mobile/web)
  • Severity classification & consequence rating
  • ICAM-aligned investigation workflow
  • Corrective actions linked to findings
  • Time-stamped evidence (photos, docs, notes)
  • Audit-ready incident-to-close trail

Outcome: Defensible incident records, regulator-ready.

2. Action & Task Governance

Purpose: Ensure risks are actioned, owned, and closed.

  • Action creation from incidents, audits, inspections
  • Role-based assignment and approvals
  • Due dates, escalation rules, and delegation
  • Evidence required for close-out
  • Cross-site visibility (site / region / group)

Outcome: No orphaned actions. Clear accountability.

3. Audit & Assurance Engine

Purpose: Replace retrospective audits with governed assurance.

  • Audit templates aligned to AU/NZ expectations
  • Scheduled and triggered audits
  • Critical control verification (CCV)
  • Evidence-first design (nothing closes without proof)
  • Findings automatically generate actions

Outcome: Continuous assurance, not point-in-time audits.

4. Governance Controls (Anchor Capability)

Purpose: Enforce authority, not automation.

  • RBAC with approval gates
  • Escalation rules with delegated authority
  • Human-in-the-loop for high-consequence events
  • Immutable evidence trails
  • Change history and decision provenance

Outcome: Decisions are traceable, defensible, and owned.

5. Reporting & Executive Outputs

Purpose: Turn governed data into leadership insight.

  • Board-ready packs (monthly / quarterly)
  • Regulator-friendly exports
  • Site / region / group dashboards
  • Trend analysis based on approved data only

Outcome: Leaders see reality, not noise.
