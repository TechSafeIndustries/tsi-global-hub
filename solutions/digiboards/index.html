How DigiBoards Fit Into the System

DigiBoards are not standalone displays. They are the visibility layer of a governed safety and compliance system.

Smart Tools (Inputs)

Sensors, wearables, and connected devices provide operational signals and leading indicators from the field.

TechSafeAI (Governance Engine)

Signals are assessed, contextualised, and routed through governed workflows with human approval and evidence trails.

DigiBoards (Visibility Layer)

Display approved information to the right people, at the right location, at the right time.

Inputs → Governance → Visibility. No automation without oversight.

Deploy DigiBoards as Part of a Governed System

DigiBoards are implemented alongside governance rules, escalation paths, and human authority.
